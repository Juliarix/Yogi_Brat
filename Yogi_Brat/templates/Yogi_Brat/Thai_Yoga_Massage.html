Thai Yoga Massage

Thai Yoga Massage is an ancient healing art from Thailand that is still practiced today. Thai Yoga Massage draws on Yoga, Ayurveda and ancient Thai healing practices. The guiding principle for Thai Yoga Massage is 'Metta' which is the pali word for 'loving-kindness.' In a Thai Yoga Massage session, a recipient can expect to be stretched, massaged and have joints passively mobilized. The client stays fully clothed and no lotion is used. There is a strong meditative component to this style of massage. People often claim feeling calmly energized after a Thai Massage and report these effects last for a couple of weeks.
